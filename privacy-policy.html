Privacy Policy for Atlas Coliseum

Effective Date: August 4, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Atlas Coliseum collects the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, age, fitness goals
  • Health & Fitness Data: Workout history, nutrition data, progress metrics
  • Device Information: Mobile device identifiers, app usage data
  • Blockchain Data: Wallet addresses and transaction data for crypto challenges

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized AI coaching and nutrition recommendations
  • Enable social features and community interactions
  • Process crypto challenge rewards and transactions
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:

  • With your consent for social features
  • For blockchain transactions (wallet addresses are public)
  • When required by law
  • With service providers who help operate our app

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including encryption and secure storage protocols.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt out of certain data collection

6. Health Information Disclaimer

Atlas Coliseum provides fitness and nutrition guidance for informational purposes only. Always consult healthcare professionals before starting new fitness or nutrition programs.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Users will be notified of material changes through the app.
